About Gnomegeddon

Gnomegeddon, The War of Gardens is the new game that has been released by the Angry Dwarf Studios that is based in the University Centre at Wakefield College.

Battle your way through three different environments saving the world from a collection of evil gnomes. There are six tanks to choose from, all with different strengths and weaknesses and five distinctive gnomes to kill while you make your way through the game.

The game is currently available for PC and can be downloaded from itch.io.
